Congaree

  • Located in South Carolina.
  • Preserves the largest tract of old growth bottomland hardwood forest left in the United States.
  • Established in 2003.

Guadalupe Mountains

  • Located in Texas.
  • The park contains Guadalupe Peak, the highest point in Texas.
  • Established in 1972.

Black Canyon of the Gunnison

  • Located in Colorado.
  • The park contains 12 miles of the 48-mile long Gunnison River.
  • Established in 1999.
